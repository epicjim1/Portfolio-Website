    I am a 2nd year computer science student at McMaster University. I am currently looking to enter the game development industry. I have always been deeply in love with the millions of stories and worlds I could explore. The ability to do and be anything, to be able to tell my own story, and to see a living and breathing world is something I knew I had to make as a child. I wish to further advance the technology and immersion for this medium, whether that be through Vr/Ar, incorporating AI, or something completely new! Feel free to check out some of my projects linked below. If you ever want to talk about development or just chat about your favorite games, send me a message to get in touch!
